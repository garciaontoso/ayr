## ¿Qué hace MAA?

Mid-America Apartment Communities, Inc. (NYSE: MAA) es **el mayor apartment REIT USA Sunbelt-focused** con sede en Memphis, Tennessee. Fundada 1977. Hoy con presencia en **300+ communities, ~100,000 units**.

### Geografía Sunbelt-pure

**Top markets**:
- **Texas** (~25%): Dallas, Austin, Houston, San Antonio
- **Florida** (~22%): Tampa, Orlando, Jacksonville, Fort Lauderdale
- **North Carolina** (~12%): Charlotte, Raleigh
- **Tennessee** (~10%): Nashville, Memphis
- **Georgia** (~10%): Atlanta
- **South Carolina, Virginia, others** (~21%)

**Sunbelt characteristics**:
- Population growth +2-3% anual (vs national +0.5%)
- Job creation tech + finance + healthcare migration
- Lower cost of living vs coastal
- Pro-business tax/regulatory environment

### Portfolio composition

- **Average unit size**: 950 sqft (larger than coastal)
- **Average rent**: $1,650/month (premium for Sunbelt but lower than coastal)
- **Average vintage**: 2010s+ (newer than peers)
- **Garden-style + mid-rise**: 90%+ suburban
- **Amenity-rich**: pools, fitness, business centers

### Estructura accionarial

- **Free float**: ~99%
- **Top institucionales**: Vanguard, BlackRock, State Street
- **Sin accionista controlador**

CEO **A. Bradley "Brad" Hill** desde 2024 — interno, ex-COO. Eric Bolton (CEO 2001-2024) ahora Chairman. **Continuidad estratégica clave**.

### El cycle Sunbelt 2020-2027

**2020-2022 boom**:
- COVID migration to Sunbelt
- Rent growth +15-25% peak
- MAA EPS $9+

**2023-2024 supply tsunami**:
- 300,000+ apartments delivered Sunbelt
- Austin specifically: 50k+ units in 1 year
- Rent growth flat to negative
- Concessions ramping (1-2 months free)

**2025-2026 absorption**:
- Last big year deliveries 2025
- 2026 absorption progress
- 5 trimestres consecutive blended rent improvement
- Bottom visible

**2027-2030 recovery**:
- New supply down 50%+
- Demand demographics sustained
- Rent growth resume +3-5% anual
- MAA EPS recovery
