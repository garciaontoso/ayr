## ¿Qué hace AvalonBay?

AvalonBay Communities, Inc. (NYSE: AVB) es **uno de los mayores apartment REITs USA** con sede en Arlington, Virginia. Fundada 1978. Hoy con presencia en 11 metros + ~89,000 units.

### Geografía focused — coastal exclusively

**11 markets premium**:
- **Northern California** (San Francisco Bay Area)
- **Southern California** (Los Angeles, San Diego)
- **New York / Mid-Atlantic** (NYC metro, Washington DC, Boston)
- **New England** (Boston metro)
- **Pacific Northwest** (Seattle)
- **Florida** (Miami, Tampa)
- **North Carolina** (Charlotte, Raleigh)
- **Denver** (selected exposure)
- **Southeast**: Atlanta selected

**Premium coastal characteristic**:
- High barriers to entry construction (zoning + cost)
- High income tenant base (median $150K+)
- Job growth tech + finance + healthcare
- Rent premium vs national average

### Portfolio composition

- **Garden-style apartments** (~50%): suburban communities
- **Mid-rise + High-rise** (~50%): urban premium
- **89,000 units total**
- **Average rent**: ~$3,000/month (premium)
- **Average occupancy**: 96%+ sostained

### Las 3 brands

- **Avalon**: premium luxury (high-rise, premium amenities)
- **AVA**: urban contemporary (millennial-focused)
- **eaves by Avalon**: value-oriented apartments

### Estructura accionarial

- **Free float**: ~99%
- **Top institucionales**: Vanguard, BlackRock, State Street
- **Sin accionista controlador**

CEO **Benjamin Schall** desde 2021 — interno, ex-COO. Continuidad estratégica desde Tim Naughton era (CEO 2012-2020).

### El driver: coastal vs Sunbelt rotation

**2020-2024 era**: Sunbelt apartments boom (Texas, Florida, Carolinas) on demographic + WFH + business migration South.

**2025+ era**: **Sunbelt oversupply** (massive new construction 2021-2024 deliveries) + **coastal recovery** (return to office + premium tenant resilience).

AvalonBay benefits from **rotation back to coastal** post-Sunbelt indigestion period.
