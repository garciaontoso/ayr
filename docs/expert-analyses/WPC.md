## ¿Qué hace W. P. Carey?

W. P. Carey Inc. (NYSE: WPC) es **uno de los REITs net lease diversified líderes** con sede en New York. Fundada 1973 por William Polk Carey. Hoy con $18.2B activos, ~120,000 properties (incluido fund-managed), **portfolio 1,400+ propiedades own-balance**.

### Estructura post-NLOP spin (2023)

**Portfolio composition** (post-cleanup):
- **Industrial (~35%)**: warehouses, manufacturing, distribution
- **Warehouse (~20%)**: logistics + e-commerce facilities
- **Retail (~25%)**: net-lease retail (essential services, automotive, restaurants)
- **Self-Storage (vendido Q1 2026)**: 11 remaining properties sold
- **Other (~20%)**: educational, hotels, restaurants, fitness

**Geographic exposure**:
- **USA (~63%)**: dominantemente USA
- **Europa (~33%)**: UK, Netherlands, Germany, France, Italy
- **Resto (~4%)**: Mexico, Canadá, others

### El trauma 2023 — spin-off + dividend cut

WPC anunció **septiembre 2023** spin-off de NLOP (National Lease Office Properties) — 59 office properties con weak fundamentals. **Cortó dividendo 19%** (de $1.071 a $0.86/Q) para reset.

**Razones del cut**:
- Office demand secular declining post-COVID
- WPC quería pure-play net lease premium
- Reset payout ratio sustainable
- Reposition portfolio for growth

**Impact accionistas**: shocked, stock cayó 30% en days. **Trauma psicológico**.

**Recovery 2024-2026**:
- 2024 AFFO $4.65 (vs $4.75 2023)
- Dividendo recovering: $0.86 → $0.87 → $0.88 → $0.89 → $0.90 → $0.91 → $0.92 → **$0.93 Q1 2026**
- Investment activity ramping
- Portfolio quality improved

### El crown jewel: net lease model

WPC owns properties + leases to single tenants:
- **15-20 year lease terms** typical
- **Tenants pay all expenses**: maintenance, taxes, insurance (true triple-net)
- **Rent escalators**: 2-3% anual contractual
- **Investment grade tenants**: 30%+ S&P investment grade

**98.1% occupied + 12.1 año WALT** = visibility cash flow long-term.

### Estructura accionarial

- **Free float**: ~99%
- **Top institucionales**: Vanguard, BlackRock, State Street
- **Carey family**: minority residual

CEO **Jason Fox** desde 2018 — interno, ejecutando turnaround post-COVID + post-NLOP spin. **Continuidad visible**.
