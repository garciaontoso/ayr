## ¿Qué hace IBM?

International Business Machines Corporation (NYSE: IBM) es **una de las empresas tecnológicas más antiguas del mundo** — fundada 1911 (CTR), Big Blue desde 1924. Sede Armonk, New York. Hoy con ~280,000 empleados, revenue ~$65B.

### Estructura post-Kyndryl spin (2021)

**1. Software (~45% revenues)**
- **Red Hat** (OpenShift Kubernetes, RHEL Linux): líder hybrid cloud
- **WatsonX** (generative AI platform): enterprise AI
- **Data + AI products**: Db2, Cloud Pak for Data, Cognos
- **Automation**: Turbonomic, Apptio, Instana
- **Security**: QRadar, Guardium, ReaQta

Margen ~30%+. Driver crecimiento.

**2. Infrastructure (~25% revenues)**
- **IBM Z mainframe** (z16, z17 generation): líder enterprise mainframe
- **IBM Storage**: enterprise storage
- **IBM Power**: AIX servers
- **Quantum computing**: leadership emergent

Margen ~22%.

**3. Consulting (~30% revenues)**
- **IBM Consulting** (post-spin Kyndryl): $20B+ revenue
- Strategy + technology + business operations
- AI implementation expertise

Margen ~10% (services typical).

### Red Hat acquisition 2019 ($34B)

IBM compró Red Hat 2019 — **la mejor adquisición tech de la década**:
- OpenShift líder Kubernetes container platform
- RHEL líder enterprise Linux
- Hybrid cloud positioning crítico
- Revenue Red Hat: $4B (2019) → **$10B+ annualized 2026**

**Justified premium pago**. Red Hat es ahora ~25% IBM revenues + driver crecimiento.

### WatsonX — el AI bet

WatsonX = **generative AI platform enterprise** lanzada 2023:
- WatsonX.ai: foundation models + tools
- WatsonX.data: data lakehouse
- WatsonX.governance: AI compliance + monitoring

**Generative AI book of business $12.5B inception-to-date** = massive enterprise traction. Cliente principales: banks, telcos, gobiernos, healthcare.

**WatsonX becoming profitable** — milestone clave.

### IBM Z mainframe renaissance

IBM Z16 (lanzado 2022) + Z17 (2025) viendo **massive demand**:
- AI inference workloads on-premise (security + low latency)
- Bank consolidation transactions
- Government workloads
- Telco BSS/OSS

Q1 2026: **IBM Z revenue +51% YoY**. Mainframe NOT dead — está renaciendo con AI workloads.

### Quantum computing

IBM Quantum es **#1 USA quantum computing** en términos de qubits + ecosystem:
- 1,000+ qubit systems
- Cloud-accessible quantum
- Quantum Network 200+ partners

Pequeño revenue today pero **option-value futuro**.

### Estructura accionarial

- **Free float**: ~99%
- **Top institucionales**: Vanguard, BlackRock, State Street
- **Berkshire Hathaway**: vendió IBM 2014-2018 (Buffett admitted error)

CEO **Arvind Krishna** desde abril 2020 — indio, ex-IBM Senior VP Cloud & Cognitive Software (architect Red Hat acquisition). **Architect transformation post-2020**.

## Moat — qué hace defendible IBM

### 1. Red Hat OpenShift hybrid cloud líder

#1 Kubernetes platform. Switching cost masivo enterprise. Multi-year contracts.

### 2. WatsonX enterprise AI

Differenciado vs ChatGPT/Claude (consumer): WatsonX es **enterprise-grade** con governance + on-premise + data residency. Banks, gobiernos, telcos prefer.

### 3. IBM Z mainframe monopoly

Largest USA mainframe vendor. Cuasi-monopolio enterprise transactions. Switching cost stratospheric.

### 4. IBM Consulting capabilities

200,000+ consultants globally. Implementation expertise = competitive advantage.

### 5. Patent portfolio massive

IBM has more US patents than any other company (recently surpassed by Samsung). Licensing revenue + protection.

### 6. Brand premium 110+ años

Big Blue brand recognition. Trust enterprise. Government contracts secure.

### 7. Quantum computing leadership

#1 USA quantum. Optionalidad future technology.

### 8. Capital allocation Krishna

Krishna ha demostrado disciplina:
- Kyndryl spin 2021: cleaner pure-play
- HashiCorp acquisition 2024 ($6.4B): infrastructure-as-code
- Apptio 2023: cloud cost management
- Buybacks consistent
- Dividend Aristocrat 31 años

- **Consulting margenes bajos** ~10%
- **Net Debt $45B** elevated post-Red Hat
