## ¿Qué hace Verizon?

Verizon Communications Inc. (NYSE: VZ) es **el mayor operador wireless USA por revenues** con sede en Nueva York. Origen 2000 (fusión Bell Atlantic + GTE). Hoy con ~110,000 empleados, revenue ~$135B.

### Estructura post-Frontier (2026)

**1. Wireless / Verizon Consumer (~70% revenues)**
- 95M+ wireless retail postpaid connections (#1 USA)
- Verizon Wireless service (premium brand)
- Verizon Total Wireless (prepaid value brand)
- Tracfone Wireless (acquired 2021) — prepaid + government Lifeline
- 5G nationwide network leadership

**2. Verizon Business (~25% revenues)**
- Enterprise wireless + wireline
- Government contracts (federal, state)
- IoT (Internet of Things)
- Edge computing
- Network managed services

**3. Verizon Consumer wireline + Frontier (~5% revenues)**
- Fios fiber broadband (legacy Verizon, ~7M subscribers)
- DSL legacy (declining)
- **Frontier fiber (acquired Q1 2026 $20B)**: 8M+ fiber passings adicionales

### Frontier acquisition — el game-changer

Verizon compró Frontier Communications $20B (closed Q1 2026):
- 8M+ fiber passings (mostly Northeast + California + Texas)
- 2M+ fiber subscribers
- Combined: Verizon ~32M fiber passings end 2026
- Combined fiber subscribers: ~9-10M
- Synergies $500M+ anuales esperadas

**Strategically critical**: Verizon era atrasado vs AT&T en fiber (AT&T 37M+ passings). Frontier acquisition cierra el gap parcialmente.

### Geografía: USA dominant

- **USA (~99% revenues)**: wireless + fiber + business
- **<1% internacional**: small wholesale + roaming

### Estructura accionarial

- **Free float**: ~99%
- **Top institucionales**: Vanguard, BlackRock, State Street
- **Sin accionista controlador**

CEO **Hans Vestberg** desde 2018 — sueco, ex-CEO Ericsson. Architect de transformation 5G + fiber + network leadership. **Continuidad estratégica fuerte**.

Verizon tiene el largest spectrum de calidad premium (mmWave + C-band + low-band combined). Network rated #1 USA in performance metrics.

### 2. Brand premium consumer

Verizon brand = "best network" reputation. Premium consumers willing to pay 5-10% more for Verizon vs T-Mobile/AT&T.

Wireless + Fiber bundled = stickiness customers + cross-sell. Convergence rate growing.

### 6. 20 años dividend growth
