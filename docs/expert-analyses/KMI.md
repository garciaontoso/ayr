## ¿Qué hace Kinder Morgan?

Kinder Morgan, Inc. (NYSE: KMI) es **el mayor operador USA de energy infrastructure pipelines** con sede en Houston, Texas. Origen 1997 (Richard Kinder + William Morgan ex-Enron co-founders). Hoy con ~10,000 empleados, revenue ~$15B.

### Las 4 categorías de productos

**1. Natural Gas Pipelines (~67% revenues, ~70% EBITDA)**
La división crown jewel:
- **79,000 millas total pipelines** (KMI total)
- **Top 5 pipelines** USA all >90% utilization
- **Volumes +8% transport, +15% gathering** Q1 2026
- LNG export feedstock (Sabine Pass, Cameron, etc.)
- Texas Intrastate Network (largest USA)
- Tennessee Gas Pipeline System
- Colorado Interstate Gas

**2. Products Pipelines (~15%)**
- Refined products (gasoline, diesel, jet fuel)
- KMP Plantation Pipeline (East Coast supply)
- CalNev (California to Nevada)

**3. Terminals (~10%)**
- Liquid + bulk storage
- Marine terminals
- Pacific Coast

**4. CO2 (~8%)**
- Carbon dioxide pipelines + EOR (enhanced oil recovery)
- McElmo Dome (largest CO2 source USA)
- Permian Basin operations

### El driver secular: USA LNG export boom

USA LNG capacity:
- 2020: 12 Bcf/d
- 2024: 14 Bcf/d
- **2027 target: 24 Bcf/d** ← masivo
- 2030+: 30+ Bcf/d posible

Cada terminal LNG necesita **pipelines feedstock natural gas**. KMI es el #1 USA pipeline operator = beneficiary directo.

### AI data centers natural gas demand

USA AI data centers consumption power:
- **2024**: ~5% USA total electricity
- **2030 target**: ~10-15%
- **Driver**: hyperscalers building data centers que consume MWh's masivos

Natural gas (peaker plants + baseload) será fuel principal expansion. KMI pipelines benefit.

### Estructura accionarial

- **Free float**: ~95%
- **Richard Kinder + family**: ~10% (founder retained)
- **Top institucionales**: BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street

CEO **Kim Dang** desde 2024 — ex-CFO + COO. Continuidad estratégica Kean era. **Steven Kean retirado 2024** (CEO 2015-2024 transformation architect).

KMI cortó dividendo **76% en diciembre 2015** ($0.51 → $0.125 trimestral) cuando crisis crudo + leverage extremo amenazaba investment grade rating. **Trauma masivo accionistas**.

## Riesgos

### 1. Net Debt elevated (probabilidad estructural)

$28B + leverage 3.5x. Limita flexibility. Cualquier downturn = pressure.

### 2. Regulación pipelines (probabilidad media)

FERC regulations + state opposition new pipelines. Delays + cancellations.

### 3. Energy transition long-term (probabilidad estructural a 20+ años)

Eventualmente natural gas demand declines. Pero horizonte 15-20+ años — KMI tiempo para adaptarse.

### 4. LNG export delays (probabilidad media)

USA LNG terminal construction can delay. KMI revenue projections depend on terminal commissioning.

### 5. CapEx execution

Project backlog $10B requires execution. Overruns possible.

### 6. Cycle natural gas pricing

Aunque KMI compensated por contracts, lower gas prices reduce growth.

### 7. Inflación commodities + steel

Pipeline construction costs sensible inflation steel + labor.

### 8. Sucesión Kim Dang reciente

Dang es CEO desde 2024. Track record building.

### 9. Founder Kinder aging

Richard Kinder 80 años. Eventual sucession plan family.

### 10. Comparativa peer Energy Transfer (ET)

ET tiene mayor yield (8%+) pero mayor risk + more controversial management.
