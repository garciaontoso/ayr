### Anexo 9: ¿Por qué Beta 0,1 es interesante (y posiblemente engañoso)?

Beta 0,1 es **anomaly** y deserves analysis:

**Possible explanations**:
1. **Low trading volume**: Beta calculation requires sufficient price movement. Low volume creates artificial low beta.
2. **Long-term contract revenue**: 70%+ of revenue from multi-year contracts means low correlation with market.
3. **State enterprise customers**: If clients are SOEs with stable budgets, revenue uncorrelated with market.
4. **Private market correlation**: Stock may move based on infrequent disclosure events, not market-wide events.

**Implications**:
- **Genuine Beta 0,1**: very defensive, attractive for portfolio risk.
- **Artificial Beta 0,1**: stock can drop dramatically when market moves.

**For HKG:9616, the truth is probably 50/50**: real defensive characteristics + statistical artifact.

**Implication for portfolio**: don't rely on beta as risk measure for this stock. Use other measures (volatility of fundamentals, downside in market shocks, etc.).
