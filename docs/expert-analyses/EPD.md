## ¿Qué hace Enterprise Products Partners?

Enterprise Products Partners L.P. (NYSE: EPD) es **el mayor MLP USA midstream** con sede en Houston, Texas. Fundada 1968 por Dan Duncan. Hoy con ~7,500 empleados, revenue ~$60B.

### Las 4 categorías de productos

**1. NGL Pipelines + Services (~50% revenues)**
- **#1 USA NGL fractionator + processor**
- Mont Belvieu Texas hub (largest NGL hub mundial)
- Natural Gas Liquids (ethane, propane, butane, isobutane, natural gasoline)
- Pipelines NGLs across USA
- Storage + terminals

**2. Crude Oil Pipelines + Services (~20%)**
- Houston Ship Channel terminals (largest USA)
- Pipelines crude oil
- Export terminals (record exports)

**3. Natural Gas Pipelines + Services (~20%)**
- Pipelines gas natural USA
- Storage facilities
- Less exposure than KMI but significant

**4. Petrochemical Services + Refined Products (~10%)**
- Petroquímica feedstock
- Refined products pipelines
- Storage + terminals

### El driver: NGLs + USA exports

USA NGL production:
- 2020: 5.2M bpd
- 2024: 6.8M bpd
- 2027 target: 7.5M bpd

USA NGL exports:
- 2020: 1.5M bpd
- 2024: 2.5M bpd
- 2030 target: 3.5M bpd+

**EPD captures premium economics** en exports + fractionation + storage.

### Mont Belvieu — el hub crítico

Mont Belvieu, Texas = **el mayor hub NGL mundial**. EPD tiene:
- 90% USA propane storage capacity
- Largest fractionation facilities (multiple trains)
- Pipeline interconnects to Permian + Eagle Ford + DJ Basin

**Cuasi-monopolio** posición — switching costs strategic + physical para customers.

### Estructura accionarial (MLP)

- **Free float**: ~70%
- **Duncan family**: ~30% (founder Dan Duncan estate)
- **Top institucionales**: BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street

Duncan family is **long-term aligned**. Sin presion vender.

Co-CEOs **A.J. "Tug" Browne** + **Randy Fowler** (since 2020). Continuidad estratégica desde Jim Teague era.

### MLP estructura (importante)

EPD es **MLP (Master Limited Partnership)** — pass-through tax structure:
- **Distributions** (no "dividends") = return of capital + ordinary income
- **K-1 tax form** annually (complicated vs 1099)
- **UBTI issue** para IRA accounts (Unrelated Business Taxable Income)
- **Best for taxable accounts** (capital gains tax-deferred until sold)
- **State filings** required if non-residence states

**For most investors**: prefiere C-corp midstream (KMI, Williams) por simplicity. EPD vale la pena si entiendes K-1.
