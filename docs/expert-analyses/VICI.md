## ¿Qué hace exactamente VICI? (didáctico)

VICI es un **REIT especializado en gaming and experiential real estate** — propietario de los edificios donde se juegan casinos, conciertos, eventos deportivos, parques temáticos, pero NO el operador que los gestiona.

### El modelo "triple-net gaming REIT"

VICI no opera casinos. Lo que hace es:

1. **Compra el real estate** (casino building + land) de operators casino que necesitan capital.
2. **Renta los buildings back** a esos operators bajo contratos triple-net (NNN) con plazos de **30+ años** y annual escalators.
3. **Cobra rents** trimestrales sin operational risk del casino.
4. **Re-invierte** en nuevas adquisiciones de casinos + experiential properties.

Casino operators agree to this structure because:
- **Releases capital**: get $1B+ from selling buildings, redeploy in operations.
- **Long-term lease security**: 30 years means they don't worry about losing the building.
- **Off-balance-sheet leverage**: lease obligation is treated differently than mortgage.

VICI gets in return:
- **Stable rent stream**: NNN structure shifts all property expenses to operator.
- **Investment-grade tenants**: Caesars, MGM, Wynn, Hard Rock, etc. — many are credit-rated.
- **Built-in growth**: 1.5-2.5% annual escalators + periodic CPI resets.

### El moat estratégico

VICI's moat is **structural**:

1. **Gaming buildings cannot be replicated easily**: 
   - Las Vegas zoning + Vegas Strip plot scarcity makes new casinos basically impossible.
   - Existing casinos are essentially monopolies on their addresses.
   - Replicating a Caesars Palace or Bellagio is impossible.

2. **Triple-net lease structure is rare in gaming**:
   - Most casino operators historically owned their buildings.
   - Sale-leaseback model in gaming is recent (2017+).
   - VICI is one of only two major operators (other is GLPI Gaming and Leisure Properties).
   - Limited competition among landlords for sale-leaseback deals.

3. **Long-term contracts**: 30-year leases mean rent visibility for decades.

4. **Inflation hedge**: CPI uplifts protect against inflation.

## ¿Qué es FFO/AFFO en gaming/experiential REITs?

For VICI:

**FFO** = Net Income + D&A − gains. Standard NAREIT.

**AFFO** = FFO − maintenance capex (very low for VICI — buildings are tenant's responsibility) − stock comp.

VICI's AFFO is essentially equal to FFO because there's almost no maintenance capex (all maintenance is tenant's obligation under NNN).

## Deep dive — la historia VICI desde 2017

VICI's history is **fascinating** for understanding why it's positioned as it is.

### Los orígenes (2017)

VICI was created from **Caesars Entertainment's Chapter 11 bankruptcy** restructuring (2015-2017).

The history:
- **Caesars Entertainment** was a giant gaming operator with massive debt ($25B+).
- 2015 file Chapter 11 to restructure debt.
- Part of restructuring: split the company into two entities:
  - **Caesars Entertainment Operating Company** (CEOC) — the operating business, casinos, hotels.
  - **VICI Properties** — the real estate subsidiary that would own the buildings/land.
- VICI emerged as an independent entity in October 2017 with:
  - 24 casino properties
  - Master lease with Caesars (then CZR) for 17+ years initial term, renewable.
  - Strong balance sheet relative to operations.
- VICI IPO'd on NYSE in January 2018 at $20.

### Growth phase 2018-2024

VICI's growth has been **extraordinary**:

**2018-2019**: 
- Acquired Greektown Casino (Detroit) for $700M.
- Caesars exercised option to lease additional facilities.
- Continued tenant relationships established.

**2020 (COVID)**:
- Las Vegas closed temporarily.
- VICI continued collecting full rent (NNN structure protected from operational decline).
- Stock briefly hit $13 lowest, recovered fully by 2021.

**2021**: 
- Acquired **MGM Growth Properties (MGP)** for $17.2B — a transformational deal.
- This brought MGM Resorts (Bellagio, Mandalay Bay, MGM Grand) properties into VICI portfolio.
- Combined entity became dominant gaming REIT.

**2022**: 
- Acquired **The Venetian Resort** Las Vegas (from Apollo Global) for $4B.
- Continued small experiential acquisitions.

**2023-2024**:
- Significant experiential expansion (Bowlero, Cabot Golf, Great Wolf Resorts).
- Investment-grade credit ratings achieved.
- Continued dividend growth.

### Current scale

By 2024, VICI is:

- 93 properties.
- ~$4B annual revenue.
- One of the top 20 REITs by mcap.
