## ¿Qué hace AbbVie?

AbbVie Inc. (NYSE: ABBV) es **una de las top-3 farmacéuticas USA** con sede en North Chicago, Illinois. Spinoff de Abbott Laboratories en enero 2013. Hoy con ~50,000 empleados, revenue ~$67B.

### Las 4 categorías de productos

**1. Immunology (~50% revenues, motor crecimiento)**
- **Humira** (adalimumab, anti-TNF): $688M Q1 2026 (-40% YoY, biosimilars)
- **Skyrizi** (risankizumab, anti-IL-23): $4.5B Q1 (+29.2%)
- **Rinvoq** (upadacitinib, JAK1 inhibitor): $2.1B Q1 (+20.2%)

Skyrizi + Rinvoq combined: **$6.6B Q1 = $26B annualized**. Ya superan Humira pico.

**2. Neuroscience (~13% revenues)**
- **Vraylar** (cariprazine, antipsychotic): $750M Q1
- **Botox** (onabotulinumtoxinA, therapeutic + cosmetic): $1.4B Q1
- **Duopa, Duvyzat**: small

**3. Oncology (~12% revenues)**
- **Imbruvica** (ibrutinib, BTK leucemia): $700M Q1 (-15% YoY, competition)
- **Venclexta** (venetoclax): $700M Q1 (+10%)
- **Epkinly** (epcoritamab, bispecific antibody linfoma): $250M Q1 (+150%)

**4. Aesthetics (~8% revenues)**
- **Botox Cosmetic**: $400M Q1
- **Juvederm** (dermal fillers): $300M Q1
- **CoolSculpting**: $50M
- Allergan acquisition 2020 ($63B legacy)

**5. Eye Care (~5%)**
- Ozurdex, Restasis (declining genéricos), etc.

**6. Other Specialty (~12%)**
- Mavyret (HCV), Creon, Synthroid (thyroid)

### Allergan acquisition 2020 ($63B)

ABBV compró Allergan junio 2020 — diversification post-Humira LOE preparation:
- Aesthetics: Botox + Juvederm
- Neuroscience: Vraylar
- Eye care
- Diversification revenue stream

**Integration ha sido OK** aunque Allergan Aesthetics underperforming inflation pricing.

### Geografía

- **USA (~75% revenues)**: dominantemente USA pricing
- **Internacional (~25%)**: Europa + Japón + Latam

### Estructura accionarial

- **Free float**: ~99%
- **Top institucionales**: Vanguard, BlackRock, State Street
- **Sin accionista controlador**

CEO **Robert Michael** desde julio 2024 — interno, ex-COO. Continuidad estratégica bajo Richard Gonzalez (CEO 2013-2024) era. Michael ejecutando Skyrizi/Rinvoq momentum.

### Pipeline post-Humira

- **ABBV-295 (amylin analog obesity)**: early Phase 1 ~10% peso loss 12 semanas. Si materializes, GLP-1 competition entry.
- **Skyrizi/anti-α4β7 combo**: Phase 2 doubled endoscopic remission Crohn's
- **Bispecific antibodies oncology**: Epkinly approved + 5 more in trials
- **CD3/BCMA bispecifics multiple myeloma**: Phase 1/2 expanding

R&D budget: $8B+ anual.
