## ¿Qué hace LyondellBasell?

LyondellBasell Industries N.V. (NYSE: LYB) es **uno de los 5 mayores fabricantes de químicos petroquímicos del mundo** con sede en Houston, Texas + Rotterdam (sede dual). Origen 2007 (fusión Lyondell + Basell). Hoy con ~19,000 empleados, revenue ~$40B.

### Las 4 categorías de productos

**1. Olefins + Polyolefins (~50% revenues)**
- **Polipropileno (PP)**: #1 mundial, 12M+ TPA capacity
- **Polietileno (PE)**: HDPE, LDPE, LLDPE
- **Ethylene + Propylene**: feedstock production

Aplicaciones: packaging, automotive, construction, médica.

**2. Intermediates + Derivatives (~20%)**
- **Propylene oxide (PO)**: #1 mundial, líder global
- **Methanol** (US Gulf Coast partnership)
- **Acetyls, oxyfuels**: para fuels + chemicals

**3. Refining (~15%)**
- Houston refinery 268k bpd capacity
- Refined products gasoline + diesel

**4. Advanced Polymer Solutions + Other (~15%)**
- Engineering plastics
- Catalyst loaders
- Recycled materials (Circulen brand)

### El cycle petrochemicals 2022-2026

**2022: peak post-COVID**
- Demand recovery + supply tight
- LYB EPS $14+
- Stock $110+

**2023-2025: severe downturn**
- China overcapacity (massive PP/PE expansion)
- Europe demand weak (Ucraina + energy crisis)
- Spreads compressed brutally
- LYB EPS dropped to <$5

**Q1 2026: green shoots**
- Crisis Middle East + supply disruption boosts margins
- Improvement vs Q4 2025 loss
- But cycle far from full recovery

**Mid-cycle target margin EBITDA**: 18% → **21%** post-transformation. Currently 12-15%.

### El dividend cut — el evento

**5 mayo 2026**: LYB anunció **dividend cut 50%**:
- Old: $1.25/Q ($5.00/year, yield 8%+)
- **New: $0.69/Q ($2.76/year, yield ~5%)**

Razones:
- Cash preservation cycle low
- Capital reallocation prioritization
- Avoid balance sheet stress
- "Improve financial flexibility"

**Trauma masivo**: LYB era held primarily for high yield. Cut destruyó investor base income-focused.

### Portfolio transformation

LYB venta 4 European assets:
- Strategy reduce exposure Europe (subscale + high cost)
- Focus North America (Marcellus advantage feedstock)
- Improve mid-cycle EBITDA margin 18% → 21%
- Free cash flow + balance sheet improvement

### Estructura accionarial

- **Free float**: ~95%
- **Top institucionales**: BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street
- **Sin accionista controlador**

CEO **Peter Vanacker** desde 2022 — belga, ex-Borealis. Ejecutando transformation post-Bob Patel era.
